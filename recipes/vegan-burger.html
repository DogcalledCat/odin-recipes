Vegan Black Bean Burger

Disclaimer: This is NOT my recipe. For the original, please go to allrecipes.com

Easily customisable vegan black bean burger

This recipe is simple to follow and can be altered in any way for any combination of flavourful vegan burgers!

Serve with you favourite fixings, dips and sides.

Ingredients

  • Black Beans - 1 Can (drained and rinsed)
  • Baby Carrots - Grated (optional)
  • Sweet onion - (chopped) 1/3 Cup
  • Green Bell Pepper - (minced) 1/4 Cup (optional)
  • Garlic (minced) - 1 Tbsp
  • Chili/Garlic Sauce (such as Sriracha, or to taste) - 3 Tbsp
  • Corn Starch - 1 Tbsp
  • Warm Water - 1 Tbsp
  • Chili Powder - 1 tsp
  • Ground Cumin - 1 tsp
  • Seafood Seasoning (such as Old Bay) - 1 tsp
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Ground Black Pepper - 1/4 tsp
  • Whole-Wheat Bread (or breadcrumbs) torn into crumbs - 2 Slices
  • Unbleached Flour - 3/4 Cup (or as needed)

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a baking sheet
  2. Mash black beans in a bowl. Add carrots, onion, bell pepper, and garlic; mix well
  3. Whisk chile-garlic sauce, cornstarch, water, chili powder, cumin, seafood seasoning, salt, and black pepper together in a separate small bowl
  4. Stir chile-garlic sauce mixture into black bean mixture; mix in bread crumbs. Stir flour, 1/4 cup at a time, into bean mixture until a sticky batter forms
  5. Spoon mounds of batter onto the prepared baking sheet, about a 3/4-inch thickness per mound; shape into burgers
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until cooked in the center and crisp on the outside, about 10 minutes per side
